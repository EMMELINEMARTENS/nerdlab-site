About Mutation festival

A conference that pushes technology - and arts education just that step further. Be inspired by international examples, discover the workshops of the future and learn how you can get started in your own organisation.

Mutation festival is the annual conclusion of the experiments on education and participation.

Experience it yourself

Become a participant of the artistic tech workshop of the future. Specialists in their field explain at a beginner level what Raspberri Pi and Algoraves means. Discover how technology can be used to make art and that doesn't have to be difficult at all.

Ontdek de experts

Bring into practice

During short presentations in small groups, experts from the field bring their experience with experimenting with tech and art education within their organization. Here you get good practices and you're able to ask all your questions.

Practical

Day structure

Between 9:30 and 6:00 pm there are lectures, workshops, discussions and the exhibition. We spoil you with coffee and a lunch, but you need tickets for the day program.

After 6 pm we open the doors for everyone free of charge. Then the expo remains open and you can enjoy Dijf Sanders and many light artists at Bring Your Own Beamer.

Location

The Chinastraat consists mainly of old factory buildings. We provide sufficient heating, but an extra sweater is a good idea.

Travel to us:
- Find the Chinastraat on Google Maps
- Come by train: get off at Gent Dampoort and walk or take bus 6
- Come by car via the R4, parking is possible on site.

Food

We provide coffee, snacks and a lunch for you at Mutation Festival.

In the evening you can enjoy delicious food from various food trucks at BYOB. Admission is free here, but your food and drinks are not.

Are there special needs in your diet? Let us know at marlies@nerdlab.be
